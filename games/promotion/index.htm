Promotion

2+ players, play until the piece is in somebody's goal.

Materials

  • A deck of playing cards or dice
  • A small item (like a Lego piece, coin, etc) to use as a game piece
  • The Promotion game board (if you don't want to print it, make one like the board shown below)

Play

If you draw a joker, place the piece on a goal and flick it into the goal it's supposed to go in. (If it's player 2's turn, flick it towards player 2's goal.) Ace is treated as a "1" card. Choose which lane you want to play on and place the piece on Start. Player 1 rolls the dice and moves the piece the number of spaces shown towards his goal. Then, Player 2 rolls the dice and moves the piece towards his goal. This will eventually get boring, so check out version 2 and version 3 for variations.

Winning

The first person who gets the piece in their goal is the winner.

Version 2

In this version, the face cards are used. This is what to do if you draw a face card:

Card What to do
Jack Invert piece position (if it is on 4, move it to -4)
Queen Move 3 spaces towards player 1's goal
King Move 3 spaces towards player 2's goal

Version 3

You have 3 cards in your hand. Play the one that, with the least number of moves, is most likely to get you to the goal. Use the face cards as described above.
