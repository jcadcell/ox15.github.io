How to play Paper Hockey

Materials


  • A table with goals (sheets of paper)
  • A puck
  • Popsicle sticks
  • Scoreboard

Play

Decide how many rounds you want to play. Flick your piece with a Popsicle stick. The first player to get the puck unto the other player's goal wins the round.
  • Use the Popsicle stick, not your hand, to move the piece
  • Don't put your hand over the center line
  • Keep your free hand off the table
